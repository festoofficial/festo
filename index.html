About Festo

Festo is a centralized college event management platform designed to streamline the creation, registration, and management of college events. With two distinct user roles - Organizers and Participants - Festo provides a seamless experience for everyone.

For Organizers

Create events, manage participants, track revenue, and generate certificates with ease

For Participants

Discover events, register quickly, make secure payments, and track your participation history
